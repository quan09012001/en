Topic 1: Describe a family member or a classmate.

My friend's name is Trung. He is 19 years old. He is single. He is a student at FPT Polytechnic, His major is IT. He likes playing games and playing football. He often plays games every night. He is tall, thin, and strong. He has short black hair. He is kind, helpful, and friendly.

We both like playing games. I really like offline games, but he likes online games.

We often play football together every weekend. I totally believe in him so when I am in trouble, I will share with him. I love his personality the most because he is wonderful.

  • His/ Her personal information (name/ age/ job/ hometown/ hobbies, marital status,…)
  • My friend's name is Trung. He is 19 years old. He is single. He is a student at FPT Polytechnic, His major is IT.
  • How do you describe her appearance? (NGOẠI HÌNH)

  • How do you describe his/ her personality? (TÍNH CÁCH)
  • He is kind, helpful, and friendly
  • What are his/her hobbies? How often?
  • He likes playing games and playing football. He often plays games every night.

  • What do you and your friend/ your father usually do together?

  • What do you love most (THÍCH NHẤT)about him/ her?
  • I love his personality the most because he is wonderful

Part 4: Các từ để hỏi

What: cái gì

where: ở đâu

when: khi nào

why: Tại sao

who: ai

what time: mấy giờ

what color: màu gì

what brand: hang nào

How: như thế nào/ phương tiện gì

how often: tần suất?(once a week/ twice a week/ three times a week)

how long: bao lâu

how far: bao xa
